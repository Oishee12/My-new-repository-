Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story are concerns Harry's conflict with Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic, and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people).

Why you should never cross Hermione Granger

2023 Dashboard

Hermione was the cleverest witch of her age, also she was be one of the scariest. Don’t believe? we couldn’t talk about Hermione without mentioning that big old brain of hers. Quite frankly, if you’re not intimidated by her intelligence, you should be. This is a witch who successfully brewed Polyjuice Potion in her second year, figured out what was lurking in the Chamber of Secrets, got a hundred and twelve per cent on her Charms exams and came up with the idea for Dumbledore’s Army. She could accomplish anything if she put her mind to it and demonstrated that repeatedly. That series honestly showed that both Harry and Ron would not have survived without Hermione’s spell. This is one witch who was as fierce as she was smart – we bet everyone that who dared to cross her everyone soon regretted it.

How the Hogwarts Professors act like a family unit

2023 Dashboard

For Harry and many of his fellow students, Hogwarts was home. But if Hogwarts was home, who was in charge? Why, the professors, of course. They each had a different role to play, almost as a family. Nobody could dispute the fact that Dumbledore was in charge at Hogwarts, not even his enemies. If Dumbledore was Hogwarts’ leader, then Professor McGonagall was definitely his second-in-command. Even before she literally marshalled troops to protect Hogwarts in the final battle with Voldemort, McGonagall was fierce in her loyalty both to Dumbledore and to the school. Professor Snape was indulgent for students in his house, Slytherin. He often turned a blind eye to Draco Malfoy’s bad behaviour but his response to Hermione was just cruel. Hagrid might not have been the most book-smart professor at Hogwarts, but if we were throwing a Hogwarts family reunion his name would definitely be at the top of the invite list. And regardless of his party garb we’re sure he’d bring the fun.
